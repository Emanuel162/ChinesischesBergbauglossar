Date Author Title Geographic Area Description Short Reference

ca. 1779 Anon. “Tongzheng quanshu zixun gechang dui” 銅政全書咨詢各厰對 Yunnan Rich in technical terms, questions answered by six local officials. TZQS 1779 DNKCTL
